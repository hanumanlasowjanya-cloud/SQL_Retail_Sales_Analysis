# 📊 Business Insights

## Revenue Analysis

- The company generated a **total revenue of 9,604,190.61** from product sales.
- **USA** generated the highest revenue (**3,273,280.05**), followed by **Spain (1,099,389.09)** and **France (1,007,374.02)**.
- **Classic Cars** was the highest revenue-generating product line, contributing **3,853,922.49** in sales.
- The **Top 10 customers** contributed a significant share of the company's overall revenue, with **Customer 141** generating the highest revenue (**820,689.54**).

---

## Customer Analysis

- **Customer 141** made the highest total payment of **715,738.98**, indicating a high-value customer.
- The analysis identified **24 customers** who have never placed an order, representing potential opportunities for customer engagement and marketing campaigns.
- Customer purchasing behavior varies significantly, with a small number of customers contributing a substantial portion of total revenue.

---

## Product Analysis

- The **1992 Ferrari 360 Spider Red (S18_3232)** was the best-selling product with **1,808 units sold**.
- The same product also generated the highest revenue within the **Classic Cars** product line.
- Each product line has a distinct top-performing product, highlighting differences in customer demand across categories.

---

## Employee & Office Analysis

- **Employee 1370** generated the highest customer revenue of **1,258,577.81**, making them the top-performing sales representative.
- **Office 4** recorded the highest total revenue of **3,083,761.58**, indicating the strongest overall sales performance among all offices.

---

## Window Function Analysis

- Ranking customers within each country helped identify the **Top 3 revenue-generating customers** for every market.
- Ranking products within each product line highlighted the highest-performing products and provided a structured comparison across product categories.

---

## Conclusion

This project demonstrates how SQL can be used to perform business-focused analysis by exploring sales performance, customer behavior, product performance, and employee productivity. The insights generated can support data-driven decisions related to sales strategy, customer retention, inventory planning, and business growth.
